a little more about Sparaluna

Sparaluna is an independent electronic producer focused on dark, emotional soundscapes.

My music moves between urban loneliness, inner fragility and the tension between human emotion and technology.

Inspired by nocturnal cities, slow movement and melancholic atmospheres, Sparaluna creates tracks that explore the space between dream and reality.
