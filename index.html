A little more about Sparaluna

Sparaluna productions focus on dark, emotional soundscapes.

My music explores urban loneliness, inner fragility, and the tension between human emotion and technology.

Inspired by nocturnal cities, slow movement, and melancholic atmospheres, Sparaluna creates tracks that exist between dream and reality.

At the moment, I am working completely independently. I handle all activities beyond music production myself, including this website, social media, and visual art.

I enjoy these activities as well, but I mainly see them as a vehicle to support my releases. My vision is to focus solely on the music in the future.

I’m also open to supporting other artists with these marketing activities.

Some words about professional activities

I currently work as a software engineer for embedded systems in automotive charge control systems.

I also bring a creative, cross-disciplinary mindset to my professional work. While my background is in embedded systems, my experience extends beyond that.

This background strongly supports my work as Sparaluna, as it allows me to realize projects like this website on my own.

Some words about my private life

I live in Brunswick and enjoy the city very much. I live there with my wife and my cat. I also have a son who recently turned 18 and visits me frequently.

Apart from my work as an artist, my hobbies include biking, jogging, hiking, and going to the gym. Sometimes I also work as a visual artist, painting pictures with watercolor and pastel chalk.
