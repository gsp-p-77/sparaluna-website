Biography

Sparaluna represents the vanguard of contemporary techno, establishing itself as an architectural project that transforms industrial sound into a hypnotic and visceral experience. With a sonic identity built on technical precision and dark narrative, the project has successfully positioned itself as one of the most compelling figures in today’s underground circuit.

Sparaluna's music is an exercise in meticulous sound design—an amalgam of acid synthesizers, immersive atmospheres, and a driving rhythmic core specifically engineered for the most demanding sound systems. This artistic vision, which shuns commercial conventions to embrace experimentation, has built a reputation for integrity and forward-thinking within the electronic scene.

The year 2026 marks a fundamental milestone in Sparaluna's career with the release of "Chasing Unit", a work that redefines his sound and expands his creative boundaries. This track was released under the prestigious Mescalina Records, one of the most influential and respected labels in global techno, renowned for its elite curation and uncompromising commitment to sonic quality.

https://mescalinarecords.wixsite.com/mescalinarecords

With "Chasing Unit" the project not only reaffirms an important place within the Mescalina Records family but also establishes itself as a project capable of balancing mechanical aggression with a unique organic sensitivity. Its steady ascent and alliance with top-tier labels ensure that Sparaluna will remain synonymous with innovation and power on the dancefloor.
